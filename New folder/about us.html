The Favourite Parking Website in Jordan

The idea for Parksee came to our founder, Saja Alkhatib, in 2025, while navigating crowded streets and struggling to find parking. Fast forward to today, and Parksee has revolutionized the way people park by allowing users to easily find, book, and pay for parking in a few taps. From bustling city centers to local businesses, Parksee offers thousands of parking spots across Jordan.

We've already helped countless drivers save time and reduce stress, and we're proud to have partnered with local space owners to make parking more accessible and profitable. With our growing user base and plans for regional expansion, we're excited to bring Parksee to more areas and become the go-to parking app for drivers across the Middle East.
